---
title: Scheduling an AI-assisted Meeting
description: How to schedule a meeting with NoteX AI assistant.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a calendar event">
    Add your meeting in Google or Outlook calendar as usual.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite NoteX bot">
    Add `bot@notex.sotatek.com` to your meeting invitees.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join your meeting">
    NoteX will automatically join, record, and transcribe.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tip

Notes and summaries arrive after each meeting in your dashboard.
