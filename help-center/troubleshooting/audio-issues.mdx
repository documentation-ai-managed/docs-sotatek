---
title: NoteX is not recording audio
description: Quick fix if NoteX is not capturing meeting audio.
---

## Symptoms

- No transcript or notes generated
- "Audio not detected" warning

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check meeting permissions">
    Ensure NoteX bot is allowed to join and record in your meeting platform (Zoom/Teams).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify microphone access">
    Host must enable recording permissions for NoteX.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reconnect NoteX">
    Remove and re-invite the NoteX bot if issues persist.
  </Step>
</Steps>
