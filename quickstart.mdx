## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have active accounts with your preferred meeting platform, such as Zoom or Google Meet. NoteX requires calendar access to join meetings automatically.

<Callout kind="info">

NoteX works with Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and more. You need admin permissions on your calendar for seamless integration.

</Callout>

## Create Your NoteX Account

Sign up in under a minute and verify your email to unlock full features.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit [https://app.notex.ai/signup](https://app.notex.ai/signup) and enter your email and password.
    
    Choose your team size during onboarding for tailored recommendations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from NoteX.
    
    Click the link to activate your account. This enables AI transcription features.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Up Profile" icon="settings">
    Log in at [https://app.notex.ai](https://app.notex.ai).
    
    Add your name, role, and time zone in the profile settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Connect Your Meeting Platform

Link NoteX to your calendar to auto-join meetings and capture notes.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Zoom" icon="video">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Authorize Calendar" icon="calendar">
        In the NoteX dashboard, go to **Integrations** > **Zoom**.
        
        Click **Connect Zoom** and sign in with your Zoom credentials.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add NoteX Bot" icon="bot">
        Generate a NoteX bot link from the dashboard.
        
        Use it to invite `notex-bot@notex.ai` to your Zoom meetings.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Google Meet" icon="google">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Grant Permissions" icon="key">
        Navigate to **Integrations** > **Google Meet** in NoteX.
        
        Approve Google Calendar access for NoteX.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enable Auto-Join" icon="play-circle">
        Toggle **Auto-join enabled meetings** in settings.
        
        NoteX scans your calendar every 5 minutes.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">

Test the connection by scheduling a test meeting. NoteX joins within 30 seconds.

</Callout>

## Schedule and Join Your First Meeting

Create a test meeting to see NoteX in action.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Schedule Meeting" icon="calendar-plus">
    Use your platform (Zoom/Google Meet) to create a 5-minute test meeting.
    
    Invite `notex-bot@notex.ai` or enable auto-join.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Meeting" icon="play">
    Join the meeting. NoteX appears as a participant and starts transcribing.
    
    Watch the real-time summary build in the NoteX dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Insights" icon="file-text">
    End the meeting. Access AI-generated notes, action items, and highlights instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
